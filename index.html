About Me

Education

I graduated from the University of the Philippines Manila which I attended from 2016-2021 with a Bachelor’s Degree in Physical Therapy. After my studies, I immediately proceeded to review for the Physical Therapist Licensure Exam (PTLE) which I then successfully completed last June 2022. I then proceeded to gain PT experience in the hospital and home-care setting for around 8 months. After which, I decided to enroll in Zuitt Coding Bootcamp as I discerned a shift in career towards the IT industry.

Exercise

Consistent exercise truly makes me a better version of myself everyday.
